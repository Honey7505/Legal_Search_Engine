NOTIFICTION
No. LAW/U.T. Merge (4) /Adapt. State Law/2022/9 5 Dated: 20/04/2022
In exercise of the powers conferred by section 19 of the Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman
and Diu (Merger of Union Territories) Act, 2019 (44 of 2019) and in pursuance of the Dadra and
Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu (Adaptation of Central Law, State Laws a nd Presidential
Regulations) Orders, dated 18th January, 2022 a copy of “The Union Territory of Dadra and Nagar
Haveli and Daman and Diu State Higher Education Council Act, 2018 as adapted to the Union
Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu ” dated 20/04/2022 is hereby publish in
the Official Gazette of this Union territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu for
implementation and for general information.
