AN ACT to amend the Mizoram Board of School Education Act, 1975
It isenacted by the Legislative Assembly of Mizoram in the sixty fifth year of the Republic of India as follows :
Short title, Extent 1 (1) This Act may be called the Mizoram Board of School Education and Commencement (Amendment) Act, 2014.
(2) It shall extent to the whole of the State of Mizoram.
(3) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by Notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
